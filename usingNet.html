Using Neural Networks

Once a Neural Network is trained (Weights calibrated) it is now able to do its task. As the example visualization of a Neural Network shows on the right-hand side a Neural Network has been calibrated with the manipulation of weights to recognize numbers. A real-world Neural Network is much more complex and can take in multiple live data at the same time, for example two camera feeds. A complex network can also be part of a larger group of Networks, this is known as a Modular neural network and can break up a large complex network into smaller more manageable networks. This is the basis of many systems, for example self-driving cars, video recommendations, weather, image recognition, image reconstruction and so on.
